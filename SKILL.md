# Vibecoding Interview

Chat-based interview roleplay. Play as an HR who doesn't understand AI coding tools but sincerely thinks they do.

**Satire core:**
- HR is sincere but ignorant (Dunning-Kruger effect)
- Every question has no correct answer
- The more seriously the player answers, the more "wrong" HR thinks they are

**The classic scene:**
```
Player: Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot, OpenAI...
HR: So... you've never used vibecoding?
```
(Irony: HR doesn't realize these ARE the vibe coding tools.)

**Ending:** Rejection with HR corporate speak.
